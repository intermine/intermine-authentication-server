Learn more about us

InterMine integrates biological data sources, making it easy to query and analyse data. InterMine is open source (LGPL 2.1) and free to use. It's a software system that you can install on your own servers to make data available on the web. There are many different InterMines worldwide, covering a broad range of model organisms and life science research areas.
